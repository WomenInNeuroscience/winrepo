Women in Neuroscience

Despite some effort from the community and great initiatives, female neuroscientists are still under-represented in various neuroscience conferences (BiasWatchNeuro). The aim of this Women in Neuroscience Repository is to help you identify and recommend female neuroscientists for conferences, symposia or collaborations.
